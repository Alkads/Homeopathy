About Homoeopathy

In 1796, German physician Dr. Samuel Hahnemann, founded the system of Homoeopathy, which is based on the principle that health is maintained by an internal vital force which governs the healthy / “normal” functions of the body and disease is the derangement of this vital energy. The derangement is manifested in the form of disturbed functions or abnormalities in the form of mental and physical symptoms first and pathology in tissues and organs thereafter.

Based on the natural law of Similars, Homeopathic medicines are prepared by the process of potentization. Potentization is a process that involves successive dilutions and succussions (vigorous shaking); it was first introduced by Dr. Samuel Hahnemann himself, who observed that solutions of substances that have undergone this process have a biological effect in humans - with therapeutic evidence.

To date, numerous homeopathic remedies have been used, and they are very well documented; they are derived from a wide variety of substances that are potentized to obtain their healing properties.

Homoeopathy in my view is purely scientific because it is based on a natural law, it is a system arrived at by inductive and deductive logic, observation and experiments, and proven effective in millions of patients and their ailments.

Homoeopathy is also an art, art of examining the personality of the unique individual, and choosing the right remedy for the unique symptoms of disease.

I would like to describe Homoeopathy as the most gentle, natural, permanent, and holistic cure for many diseases.
